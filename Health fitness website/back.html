Back Exercises

Build a strong and defined back with these effective exercises

Back Exercise Library

Pull-ups

Target: Lats, Rhomboids, Biceps

Difficulty: Intermediate

Classic bodyweight exercise for building upper back strength.

Bent-over Rows

Target: Lats, Rhomboids, Middle Traps

Difficulty: Intermediate

Compound movement for building overall back thickness.

Lat Pulldowns

Target: Lats, Rhomboids

Difficulty: Beginner

Great alternative to pull-ups for building lat width.

Deadlifts

Target: Entire Posterior Chain

Difficulty: Advanced

King of all exercises for building overall back strength.

Face Pulls

Target: Rear Delts, Upper Traps

Difficulty: Beginner

Excellent for improving posture and rear delt development.
